Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is BlinkWell's risk prediction?

Our AI model has been trained on extensive medical data and achieves high accuracy rates. However, our predictions are for informational purposes and should not replace professional medical advice.

Is my personal health data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and comply with healthcare privacy regulations. Your data is never shared without your explicit consent.

Can I use BlinkWell if I already have eye problems?

BlinkWell can help track your progress and provide lifestyle recommendations, but please consult with your eye care professional for specific medical conditions.

How often should I update my information?

We recommend updating your lifestyle information monthly and uploading new fundus images during regular eye exams for the most accurate predictions.

What's included in the free version?

The free version includes basic risk assessment, daily habit tracking, and access to our AI chatbot. Premium features include detailed progress analytics and personalized recommendations.
